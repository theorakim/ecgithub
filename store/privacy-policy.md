# Privacy Policy — ECGithub

**Last updated:** 2026-03-20

## Data Collection

ECGithub does **not** collect, store, or transmit any user data.

## What the extension accesses

- **GitHub contribution graph**: The extension reads the publicly visible contribution data on GitHub profile pages to generate the ECG visualization. This data is processed entirely within your browser and is never sent to any external server.
- **Chrome storage**: Your display preferences (color theme, animation toggle, etc.) are stored locally using `chrome.storage.sync`, which syncs across your Chrome browsers via your Google account. No third party has access to this data.

## Third-party services

ECGithub does not use any analytics, tracking, or third-party services.
